About the developers

Our team at Coding Ducks is comprised of four Ducks from Stevens Institute of Technology: Caleb Choy, John Cinquegrana, Megha Mansuria and Leah Mitelberg. Inspired by Duckhacks 2019, we decided to create an interactive site for young children to learn how to code!
