Welcome to the Duck Grid Game

There are four commands to this game: up, down, left, and right.
An example solution would be to get the duck to move 2 spaces up and 1 space left by inputing the command "up up left" into the submission box then pressing the submit button. If you feel like trying out something a little harder, click the "Next Level" Button lower down on the page.
